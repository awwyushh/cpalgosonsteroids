## Practical Constants (CPP)

Rough operation counts per second on a modern CPU:
- Simple operations (add, compare): ~$10^9$
- Cache-friendly array access: ~$5 \times 10^8$
- Random memory access: ~$10^7$–$10^8$
- Cache miss: ~100× slower than cache hit

**Contest rule of thumb**: $10^8$ operations/second. Plan your solution accordingly.
