## Learning Path

**Start here if new to probability in CP:**
1. [Basic Probability](/math/probability/basic-probability) — sample spaces, conditional prob, Bayes
2. [Expected Value](/math/probability/expected-value) — linearity is the key tool
3. [Probability DP](/math/probability/probability-dp) — combine expectation with DP

**For randomized algorithm analysis:**
4. [Randomized Algorithms](/math/probability/randomized-algorithms) — Monte Carlo vs Las Vegas
5. [Hashing](/math/probability/hashing-probability) — universal hashing theory
6. [Coupon Collector](/math/probability/coupon-collector) — occupancy problems

**For advanced stochastic analysis:**
7. [Markov Chains](/math/probability/markov-chains) — stationary distributions, mixing
8. [Random Walks](/math/probability/random-walks) — optional stopping, Pólya
