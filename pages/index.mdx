# CP Algorithms on Steroids

**The most comprehensive Competitive Programming reference ever built.**

Covers everything from Codeforces Div.4 basics to ICPC World Finals and IOI gold-level topics.
Built as a learning platform _and_ a contest reference handbook.

---

## What Is This?

A brutally complete encyclopedia of **Competitive Programming Mathematics** and **Data Structures** — structured for progressive learning, contest preparation, and on-the-fly reference during training.

Think: cp-algorithms + USACO Guide + KACTL + OI Wiki + ICPC Notebook, but unified, improved, and extended.

**Covers:**
- Every mathematical tool used in competitive programming
- Every data structure from beginner to research level  
- Proofs, implementations, complexity analyses, and contest patterns
- Curated problem ladders from warmup to legendary
- Learning roadmaps for every competition tier

## Why This Exists

Existing resources are excellent but fragmented:
- **cp-algorithms**: great explanations, missing many contest tricks
- **USACO Guide**: US-centric, limited math depth
- **KACTL**: template-focused, no teaching
- **OI Wiki**: Chinese-primary, translation gaps
- **Codeforces blogs**: scattered, inconsistent quality

This handbook consolidates, extends, and improves all of them.
